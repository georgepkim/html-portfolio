Top 5 Marvel Movies

These are the top 5 movies from the MCU according to me

  1. Doctor Strange Multiverse of Madness

    2. This movie released in 2022 is the second Doctor Strange movie and opens the door to the multiverse in the MCU, has some amazing cameos and great sfx.

  2. Guardians of The Galaxy vol 3

    3. This is the last entry of the Guardians of the Galaxy trilogy directed by James Gunn, its an emotional rollercoaster that gives some kind of closure to the heroes.

  3. Spider Man No Way Home

    4. The last part of the Spider Man trilogy starring Tom Holland since the Sony partnership with Marvel, it's a movie designed to please three different generations and does it well.

  4. Avengers Infinity War

    5. The third Avengers movie that brings together heroes from three different Marvel phases and finally shows the big bad that has been teased for ten years and almost 20 movies, the ending shows an unexpected twist that left fans eager for the next installment.

  5. Avengers Endgame

    6. The closure of the infinity saga and almost 20 films over ten years that brings a satisfying yet bittersweet end to the biggest names in the MCU, it's not an overstatment to say this is the greatest crossover of all times.
